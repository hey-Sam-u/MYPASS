Privacy & Security — Simple, honest, strong

Your files are yours. We help store encrypted bytes — only you can unlock them.

How it works (fast)

  1. You pick a passphrase in your browser.
  2. Your files are encrypted in the browser (AES-GCM) before upload.
  3. We store only encrypted blobs, salts, and metadata — never your passphrase.
  4. To view/download, you supply the passphrase and decryption happens in your browser.

What we collect

  • Account email (to identify your vault).
  • Encrypted file blobs, filename, file type, size, upload timestamp.
  • Salt & IV (needed to decrypt client-side) — stored, never secret by themselves.

Security highlights

  • Client-side encryption: AES-GCM + 256-bit keys.
  • Passphrases are never sent to or stored on our servers.
  • Authentication: passwords hashed with bcrypt; sessions via short-lived JWTs.
  • Transport: HTTPS only. Server access limited & logged.
  • Backups: encrypted, limited retention, restricted access.

What we DON'T do

We never read or sell your file contents. We do not keep plaintext copies of your files or passphrases. If you lose your passphrase, we cannot decrypt your files — that’s by design.

Limitations & honest note

No system is absolutely risk-free. We work to reduce risk (strong crypto, secure servers), but we cannot promise a 100% guarantee. If you keep weak passphrases or reuse passwords elsewhere, your account can still be compromised — protect your passphrase.

Your rights

  • Delete your account — all encrypted data removed.
  • Request export of metadata or encrypted blobs (we'll provide a downloadable archive).
  • Contact us for support or legal requests (details below).
